Our Programmes

Learning Pathways for Every Stage

From the curiosity of early childhood to the leadership of teen years, Bishop Sackey Academy guides students with excellence, care, and joy.

Creche - KG

Pre-School

Play-based discovery, language immersion, and nurturing routines spark a love for learning from the start.

  • Montessori-inspired centres and sensory play labs
  • Phonics, numeracy games, and outdoor adventures
  • Weekly music, movement, and faith moments
Basic 1– 6

Primary

Rigorous academics combine with creative projects, technology integration, and collaborative problem-solving.

  • Cambridge-aligned numeracy & literacy blocks
  • Inquiry projects in science and social studies
  • Creative arts, coding, and club experiences
Basic 7– 9

Junior High

Students transition into confident scholars with leadership opportunities, exam readiness, and service learning.

  • Subject specialists and exam preparation clinics
  • STEM labs, debate, and entrepreneurship modules
  • Career guidance and mentorship programmes
Beyond the classroom

A supportive pathway from early years to junior high

Our learning journey is carefully sequenced so that every milestone builds on the last. Families stay connected through regular feedback, home learning partnerships, and community celebrations.

  • Character & Leadership

    House mentorship, prefect council, and service projects nurture resilience, empathy, and a heart for others.

  • Continuous Assessment

    Blended assessments, progress trackers, and parent-teacher conferences ensure personalised support for every learner.

  • Enrichment & Support

    After-school clubs, remedial coaching, and enrichment clinics help students explore passions and strengthen core skills.
